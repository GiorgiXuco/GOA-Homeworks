E 28

The BMW E28 is the second generation of BMW 5 Series executive cars, which was produced
from 1981 to 1988 and replaced the E12 5 Series. Pre-series production began in April 1981
and the car was introduced in the autumn that year.

E 28 Engine

The E28 was the first 5 Series produced with diesel engines (the 524d and 524td models)
and with a petrol engined model specifically aimed at increased fuel economy (525e/528e model).

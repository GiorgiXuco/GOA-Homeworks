E 60

The fifth generation of the BMW 5 Series executive cars consists of the BMW E60 (saloon version)
and BMW E61 (wagon version, marketed as 'Touring'). The E60/E61 generation was produced by
BMW from 2003 to 2010 and is often collectively referred to as the E60.

E 60 v10

The M5 model of the E60 generation was introduced in 2005 and produced in saloon and wagon body styles.
The E60 M5 is powered by the BMW S85 V10 engine, which produces 373 kW (500 hp) at 7,750 rpm, 520 N⋅m
(384 lb⋅ft) at 6,100 rpm and has a redline of 8,250 rpm. The 0–100 km/h (0–62 mph) acceleration time is 4.7 seconds.

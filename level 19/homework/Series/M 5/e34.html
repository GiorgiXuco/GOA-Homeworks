E 34

The BMW E34 is the third generation of the BMW 5 Series, which was produced from 2 November
1987, until 1996. Initially launched as a saloon in January 1988, the E34 also saw a "Touring" station
wagon (estate) body style added in September 1992, a first for the 5 Series. BMW replaced the E34
with the E39 5 Series in December 1995, although E34 Touring models remained in production until June 1996.

F 90

The F90 M5 is based on the G30 5 Series and uses an all-wheel drive ("xDrive") powertrain,
being the first time that an M5 has not been rear-wheel drive. However the all-wheel drive system
is biased towards the rear wheels. It can also be configured to send power to the rear wheels only,
if the electronic stability control (ESC) is disabled. The transmission is an 8-speed ZF GA8HP75Z automatic.

F 90 Engine

The engine is an evolution of the S63 from the previous generation. With a power output of 441 kW (600 PS; 591 hp)
the new M5 has the same power as the limited edition "Competition Package" and "30 Jahre M5" models of the previous
generation, while the torque has been raised to 750 N⋅m (553 lb⋅ft), 70 N⋅m (52 lb⋅ft) more than in the previous M5.
